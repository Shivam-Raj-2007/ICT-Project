Folk Dances Of Bihar


Folk dances are one of the most interesting aspects of Bihar’s culture. Most of the folk dances reflect common life, people’s sorrows, accomplishments, and problems. On important social gatherings, these dances are performed in group with musical instruments like table, dholak and harmonium accompanying the singers.

    The 8 popular forms of folk dances of Bihar

  1. Bidesia
  2. Jat-Jatin Dance
  3. Jhijhia dance
  4. Jumari Dance
  5. Kajari Dance The pleasant change that has come due to the rainy season
  6. Sohar-Khilouna Dance
  7. Fagua
  8. Domkach
